Vegan Brownies Recipe

Description

Easy, delicious recipe for brownies. Prep time of 25 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Cocoa powder
  • Baking powder
  • Ground flax
  • Sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Vegan butter
  • Chocolate chips
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Stir ground flax with water to make egg like mixture. Set aside.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Line the pan with parchment paper.
  3. Whisk melted butter, sugars, and the flax. Mix well.
  4. Sift in flour and cocoa powder to mixture. Add salt and baking powder. Stir until just combined.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Pour batter into pan.
  7. Bake for 35-40 minutes.
  8. Let cool before slicing into squares.
