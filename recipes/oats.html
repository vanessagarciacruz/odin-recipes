Overnight Oats Recipe

Description

Simple, easy recipe for breakfast with only four ingredients. Prep time of 10 min. 12 grams of protein per serving.

Ingredients

  • Rolled oats
  • Almond milk
  • Chia seeds
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Place 1/2 cup of oats in container.
  2. Place 1/2 cup of almond milk in container.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of chia seeds.
  4. Mix well.
  5. Add honey to liking, and mix again.
  6. Garnish with berries, bananas, almonds, and peanut butter.
