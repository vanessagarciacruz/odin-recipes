Tofu Stir Fry Recipe

Description

Protein-packed tofu stir fry with a delicious glaze. 20 min prep time.

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Bell peppers or veggies of choice
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Canola oil
  • Minced garlic
  • Green onions
  • Chili paste (optional)

Steps

  1. Drain tofu. Cut into small cubes.
  2. Add oil into pan. Once oil is hot, add tofu and drizzle on soy sauce.
  3. Stir tofu every now and then. Cook until tofu reaches a golden color.
  4. Add garlic, green onion, soy sauce, and chili paste if wanted.
  5. Add chosen vegetables and cook until soft, for about 8 minutes.
  6. Serve over rice and garnish with green onions.
